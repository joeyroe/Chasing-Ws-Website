Privacy Policy

I, Joey, am the app designer of Chasing Ws, I am also the designer of this very website. I myself value my privacy, and I also very much value the privacy of anybody that uses Chasing Ws the app and or this website. I can tell you that as the designer/creator, I designed Chasing Ws the app and this website to not collect any of your personal data. The only data that is saved in this game is your high-score. In the app your high-score is saved to your device, and on this website your high-score is saved to the browser. If you send me an email or contact me via social media, I may keep a copy of that.

I did use a game engine called 'Flame' to build Chasing Ws the app. If you'd like to know more about it here's the link:
https://docs.flame-engine.org/latest/

Policy Updates:
I may make changes to this privacy policy. Any changes will be updated here.
